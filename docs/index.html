Accidents in Washington State

Data was retrieved from this source. The data collected was on traffic accidents in forty-nine states in the US and filtered for only traffic accidents in the state of Washington. Data dates range from February 2016 to December 2019 and was aggregated via mainly two API's, MapQuest and Bing, which obtained data reported from federal and state departments of transportation, law enforcement agencies, traffic cameras, and traffic sensors. The data displayed on the above map are based on the longitude and latitude coordinates and can be interactively filtered, via the sliders, on the time of day and temperature with the colors indicating the severity of the traffic accident.
